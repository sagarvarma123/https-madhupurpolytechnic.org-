 Our Vision:
We at Madhupur Polytechnic will impart futuristic technical education and instill high patterns of discipline through our dedicated staff, who shall set high standards, making our students technologically super and ethically strong, who in turn shall improve the quality of life of the humane race.



Our Mission:
Our mission is to educate students from all over Jharkhand, including those from the local and rural living standards of their families, industry and society. We will provide individual attention, quality education and take care of character building.



Admission:
Admission in to Madhupur Polytechnic will be as per state Govt. norms through councelling process conducted by Govt. of Jharkhand. 43% seats are free seats & 57% seats are payment seats. For free seats tuition fee (Except hostel fee will be same as that of Govt. Polytechnic). For payment seats fee structure will be charged according to fee fixation committee Govt. of Jharkhand
